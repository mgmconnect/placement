About Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell of our college plays a crucial role in guiding and providing complete assistance to all the UG and PG passing out students for their entry into suitable employment and successful career. The Placement Cell functions round the year to facilitate contacts between the students and the industries. We ensure that the students are trained according to the needs of the companies before they participate in the campus recruitment . The Placement Cell conducts career guidance programmes for all the registered students. The cell organises various training programmes including Mock exams and Interviews, GD, Communication Skills, Soft skills, seminars and Workshop etc in order to meet the requirements of the job industries. The cell also invites HR Managers from different industries to conduct training and placement programmes for all the students.
